Apply for a Blue Badge

Use this service to apply for a Blue Badge if you have a disability or health condition that affects your mobility.

You will need:

  • proof of identity (birth certificate, passport or driving licence)
  • proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)
  • evidence of your disability or health condition
  • a recent digital photo

It takes around 15 minutes to complete.
